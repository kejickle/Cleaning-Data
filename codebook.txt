"Subject"
	Number assigned to each subject.  Integer 1-30. 

"Activity" 
	Assigned to each subject for each activity they tested.  Factor with 6 levels with the following labels. 
	1 WALKING
	2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
	3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	4 SITTING
	5 STANDING
	6 LAYING
